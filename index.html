Background.

Given all the hype around the legality of Uber in many major cities, it leads one to wonder if its truly profitable to jump into the hot seat and drive one. It has also brought up many discussions on how it affects the livelihood of legalised taxi operators. If you are curious about the life of a taxi operator or have thoughts to become one, join me and sit tight for the ride.
