Background.

With all the hype around the legality of Uber in many of the cities that it operates in, it led me to think about what truly goes on in the day-to-day life of the taxi operators that compete against it.

We're in luck here though. Join me on a data driven journey into the lives of the iconic NYC Medallion drivers!

Show Me The Data

The data was initially retrieved by Chris Whong, a data visualization analyst, through a FOIL (Freedom of Information Law) request. In its entirety, it came in at a whopping 18.7gb when zipped.

Fortunately, the internet is full of amazing people and the dataset met Google engineer Jason Hall. It now lives happily as a Google Big Query project.
